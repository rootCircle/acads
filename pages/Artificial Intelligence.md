- ### BFS
	- Uninformed, Complete(searches all even in infinite)
	- Uses FIFO (Queue) to traverse
	- O(V+E) O($b^d$)
	- Optimal (cost is min)
- ### DFS
	- Incomplete (infinite/cycle)
	- Stack (LIFO)
	- Non-optimal (cost may not be min)
	- O(V+E) O($b^d$)
